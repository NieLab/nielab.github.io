Research Topics in Mathematical, Computational, and Systems Biology

We seek to understand the mechanisms that underlie complex biological systems through a combination of modeling, analysis, and the development of computational tools. We develop models to describe particular cellular or subcellular systems tightly coupled to experimental data. The pages below describe some of our current research foci.

Systems Biology

  • Stem Cell Decision Making
  • Epithelial to Mesenchymal Transition
  • Noise in Developmental Patterning
  • Single Cell Fate Dynamics
  • Spatio-temporal Dynamics of Gene Expression

  • Network Inference

  • Numerical Methods for Stochastic Differential Equations
