MCSB Resources

    The Mathematical, Computational and Systems Biology doctoral program at UCI trains budding scientists and mathematicians to think about biology from an engineering and mathematical perspective. For more details please visit the MCSB main page.

CMCF Resources

    The NSF-Simons Center for Multiscale Cell Fate (CMCF) is focused on understanding cell fate and how biology "scales" from biochemical events to cells to tissues. For more information please visit the CMCF main page.
